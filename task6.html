Task 6: Cel Shading - Model colors

We imported a model of the Darknut Sword from here. In this task, we factored in the color of imported models into our toon shading algorithm. The color of the model at a given vertex is given by a texture map at location (u, v). (u, v) is provided in the vertex shader, so just needed to pass in texture maps for each component (child) of the model. We did this by looping through each child of the model and retrieving its texture map.
