Task 3: Cel Shading - Toon shading

We refer to this video by Jasper to gain a grasp of how cel shading was done in Zelda: The Wind Waker, which pioneered the shading method. We adapted the "toonification" part of the shading to a scale from 0 to 1 instead of 0 to 255, as suggested in the video, because it follows three.js convention. We opted to use the small range from 0.47 to 0.53 luminosity, which we found by using the 120/255 and 136/255 values that Jasper mentions in the video to stay true to the aesthetic of cel shading as seen in Zelda: The Wind Waker. Compared to the previous task, one will see the difference in the amount of shape that is illuminated by the light with a rather rapid (but still gradual) change in light and dark portions of each shape. All luminosities that were less than or equal to 0.47 were black and those that were greater than or equal to 0.53 were white.
